Information of Us

TOP is a youth organization mainly focused on SDG 4 among 17 global goals, which is quality education; to give priority to education for all.

In the TOP organization logo, the hiker represents youths who are trying to reach the top of the mountain without giving up in this situation.

With the slogan, "Climb To The Top With TOP", we started everything with the desire to give a hand to those youths to reach the top of the mountain and become toppers in their interested fields.

Every age; youngsters and elders who wish to reach the top of the mountain, are invited to keep an eye on our TOP organization which will stand for many years.
