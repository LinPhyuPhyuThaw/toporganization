Project Management

Are you someone who likes being organized and eager to make a difference? We oversee initiatives that positively impact our community and your skills will play a vital role in ensuring projects run smoothly.

Public Relations

Founder

Shoon Lae Nandar Zaw

Shoon Lae Nandar Zaw is an engaged youth community activist currently pursuing her secondary education. With a wealth of over two years' experience in various volunteer capacities, she exhibits exceptional proficiency in internal operational management and public speaking. She deeply empathizes with starving people and orphans and is working on developing TOP into an organization which acts as a stepping stone for kids and youths in Myanmar to continue their dreams and education.

“What's stopping you? Start working now.” -Shoon Zaw-




Founder

Eaint Kyal Sin

Eaint Kyal Sin is a GED graduate who is dedicated to helping students achieve their academic goals. She has been actively involved in community work since the age of 14 and offering free educational services such as mentoring essays and opening free classes through her page, Roll Number 41. She is deeply passionate about education, gender equality, and environmental sustainability. Eaint mentioned that she started TOP with the desire to build the country with education.

Honors and Achievements - Currently participating in the Global Climate Science Olympiad Quiz 2024 at the semi-final - First prize winner of the “Dream” Video Competition - Winner of the “Gender Equality and Our Society” Video Competition - Second prize winner of the “International Women's Day” Video Competition
